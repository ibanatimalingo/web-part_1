ABOUT US

The story behind the studio.

HOW IT STARTED.

Apex Auto Designs was founded in 2015 by two automotive obsessives who believed that car design had become too predictable. They wanted to build something different — a studio where imagination had no limits and every commission was a blank canvas.

Starting in a small garage in Johannesburg, the studio quickly earned a reputation for bold, fearless designs that turned heads at shows across South Africa and beyond.

Today, Apex operates from a state-of-the-art design facility and has completed over 150 bespoke builds for clients across the continent.
